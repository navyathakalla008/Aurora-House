Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I volunteer at Aurora House?

+

We welcome volunteers! You can fill out our contact form selecting "Volunteer Opportunities" or email us at volunteer@aurorahouse.org. We offer various volunteer roles including tutoring, mentoring, event support, and administrative assistance.

Can I visit Aurora House?

+

Yes! We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a visit. We conduct tours on weekdays and some Saturdays. All visitors must complete a background check for the safety of our children.

How do I sponsor a child?

+

Child sponsorship is a wonderful way to make a direct impact. Contact us through our form or email donate@aurorahouse.org. We'll match you with a child and provide regular updates on their progress.

Are donations tax-deductible?

+

Yes! Aurora House is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.

What items can I donate in-kind?

+

We accept new or gently used items including clothing, books, toys, school supplies, sports equipment, and non-perishable food. Please contact us before dropping off items to ensure we can accept them.
