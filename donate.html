Why Your Donation Matters

Every contribution, no matter the size, directly impacts the lives of children at Aurora House. Your donation helps us provide:

  • 🏠 Safe and comfortable housing
  • 🍽️ Nutritious meals and snacks
  • 📚 Educational resources and supplies
  • 🏥 Healthcare and medical services
  • 👕 Clothing and personal care items
  • 🎨 Arts, sports, and recreational activities
  • 💼 Career guidance and life skills training
