About Aurora House

Our Story, Our Mission, Our Commitment

Our Story

Aurora House was founded in 1999 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide every child with a safe, loving home and the opportunity to thrive. What started as a small initiative has grown into a beacon of hope for hundreds of children.

Named after the aurora - the beautiful natural light that illuminates the sky - we believe every child deserves to shine brightly, regardless of their circumstances. Our founders, a group of dedicated social workers and community leaders, recognized the urgent need for quality care and support for orphaned and vulnerable children.

Over the past 25 years, we have created a nurturing environment where children receive not just shelter and food, but love, education, healthcare, and the tools they need to build successful futures. We've seen children grow into confident, educated, and compassionate adults who continue to make positive impacts in their communities.
