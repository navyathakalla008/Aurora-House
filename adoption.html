Adoption Program

Our Adoption Process

A step-by-step guide to welcoming a child into your family

1

Initial Inquiry

Submit an adoption inquiry form and attend an information session to learn about our adoption process and requirements.

2

Application & Documentation

Complete the adoption application and submit required documents including background checks, financial statements, and references.

3

Home Study

Our social workers will conduct a comprehensive home study to ensure your home is safe and suitable for a child.

4

Matching & Meeting

We'll match you with a child based on compatibility. You'll receive detailed information including health reports and videos before meeting.

5

Pre-Adoption Visits

Gradual introduction through supervised visits to help both you and the child adjust to the new family dynamic.

6

Placement & Support

Final placement with ongoing support, counseling, and resources to ensure a successful transition for everyone.

Adoption Requirements

What you need to know before starting the adoption process

✅

Age Requirements

Adoptive parents must be at least 21 years old. There is no maximum age limit, but health and ability to care for a child are considered.

🏠

Stable Home

A safe, stable living environment with adequate space for a child. Home study will assess safety and suitability.

💰

Financial Stability

Proof of financial stability to provide for a child's needs. Income requirements vary based on family size.

❤️

Commitment

Demonstrated commitment to providing a loving, permanent home. Willingness to participate in training and support programs.

🔍

Background Check

Complete background check including criminal history, child abuse registry, and reference checks.

📚

Training

Completion of pre-adoption training covering child development, trauma-informed care, and adoption-specific topics.
